# Ethics

Huddle is designed as facilitator support, not automated political persuasion.

- Mediation, not manipulation: the system suggests structure and questions, not conclusions.
- Explainable routing: every group includes a reason for why participants were assigned together.
- Minority voice protection: the scoring layer penalizes isolated minority viewpoints.
- Facts vs. values: mediation prompts separate evidence disputes from priority disputes.
- Human control: organizers can inspect the graph, groups, and risk scores before using them.
- Privacy: the MVP avoids protected identity inference and uses only self-submitted statements.
